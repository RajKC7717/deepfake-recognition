Privacy-First by Design

Summary

We never see your video. All deepfake analysis runs entirely inside your browser using on-device AI. No video frames, images, or personal data are transmitted to any server by default.

What We Collect

By default, this extension collects nothing. Optional features:

Anonymous usage statistics (opt-in): If you enable this in Settings, we collect only aggregate, non-identifiable counts such as "detection ran 5 times today". No video data, no IP addresses, no user identifiers are included.

Backend Validator (opt-in): If you enable the self-hosted backend feature, individual frames are sent to your own server. Anthropic / the extension authors never receive this data.

Video Processing

Video frames are processed using TensorFlow.js running entirely within the Chrome extension's offscreen document. Frames are analyzed in memory and discarded immediately after inference. They are never written to disk or transmitted over the network.

Permissions Explained

tabCapture: Required to read the video stream of the active tab for analysis. This is the core functionality of the extension.

activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the currently open tab (e.g. detect video elements in Google Meet).

storage: Saves your settings (sensitivity, FPS, etc.) locally in Chrome. These never leave your device.

scripting: Injects the overlay UI into Google Meet pages.

offscreen: Creates a hidden document to process the video stream without affecting your active tab's performance.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sold or shared.

Data Retention

All in-memory data (video frames, detection scores) is discarded when you stop detection or close the tab. Settings stored via chrome.storage.sync remain until you uninstall the extension or clear extension data in Chrome settings.
